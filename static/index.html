Bay Area Programming Competition

Who Are We?
The Bay Area Programming Contest (BAPC) is a programming contest for contestants of all levels designed by Gunn's Competitive Programming Club. We hope to give Bay Area high school students an opportunity to compete with peers and solve some interesting problems in an ICPC style format!

To see what our problems look like, Check out our resource page!
Frequently Asked Questions
Where will BAPC be held?
BAPC will be held in the Gunn High School Library, however there will be an online mirror for those who can't make it.
How do I register?
Registration is not open yet. Check back later!
What is the format of the contest?
The contest will be a 5 hour long ICPC style contest. You will be given 10 problems to solve with your team.
What is the team size?
The team size is 3.
